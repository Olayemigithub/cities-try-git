Tokyo

Tokyo is known for being a lively and futuristic metropolis. It features a unique mix of the old
modern that blends perfectly with ancient temples hidden among large, modern architecture.

Over 14 million foreigners visit Tokyo each year. It has been a well-traveled destination for decades.
Cherry blossoms, Mt. Fuji, and sushi grace the postcards and photographs.
There are lots of fun things to do in Tokyo, even for the little ones.
